Yellow Vests!

The Gilet Jaunes, translated meaning Yellow Vests, are a group of protesters in france. The movement formed online in social media and is comprised of a large collection of individuals from all different backgrounds. The Gilet Jaunes are individuals from the working and middle class however they come from all different ages and professions. They are nationalistic and intend to wreak havoc. This is common in France and the citizens feel it is inalienable right to protest. Expressing ideas like free Tommy Robinson, a very nationalistic activist located in britain, these nationalistic ideas fight western culture in order to preserve there own. Throughout France the revolution is only seen in a positive light celebrating Bastille Day and the general liberation that has come from Revolution. Nationalism is important in france and no group expresses these ideas better the the Gilet Jaunes.

The Gilet Jaunes have produced some of the largest protests in the history of France. The group formed fighting the taxation with a focus on increased diesel tax. As time has progressed the issues have spread as the group gains in popularity. Seeking reassurance from people like Tommy Robinson, a british nationalist, to support there nationalistic ideas. The group consists of people of french origin as you may notice in the photo above all have the same ethnic appearance. The group has racist undertones using taxation as an excuse to unify and discriminate. Fighting for unity and brotherhood the group is indirectly attacking those of international origin striking down their ability to be successful in France.

The nationalistic Ideas of the group have existed in France since it revolution, the Gilet Jaunes are simply giving it a stage amplifying it's voice. The violent clashes and protests that have occurred across the city have been rampant. However now the intent of the group has become murky and it is unknown what issue will attract them next.
